How it began

From a Kitchen Table to a Dream

Halo Buds started in 2021 as a side passion project — handcrafting floral accessories for friends and family who couldn't find anything truly unique and personal on the market.

What began with a handful of floral crowns for a friend's wedding quickly blossomed into something far bigger. Word spread, orders came in, and we realized we had built something special: a brand where every single piece is made by hand, with intention and love.

Today, we serve thousands of customers across India, but our heart remains the same — creating beautiful, customizable pieces that make people feel seen and celebrated.

Our founder, Kavya, still hand-selects every flower and material, ensuring the quality and heart that started Halo Buds continues in every creation.
